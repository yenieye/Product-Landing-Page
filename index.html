All about Oreo

  • Created on March 6, 1912
  • Oreo is an American sandwich cookie
  • Manufactured by National Biscuit Company (Nabisco)
  • The cookies are vegan and kosher
  • "Oh! Oh! Oreo" was the first slogan made (1950)
  • Lemon cream was the first Oreo flavor
  • Known as "Milk's Favorite Cookie" (2014)
